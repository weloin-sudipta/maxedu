# Future Scope

Maxedu is a growing project with a long-term vision of transforming the academic and library experience.

## Planned Capabilities
- **Mobile Application**: A dedicated mobile app for students and teachers to access the library on the go.
- **RFID Integration**: Seamless book tracking and inventory management through RFID technology.
- **E-Library Support**: Integration with digital content providers for e-books and virtual resources.
- **Advanced Predictive Analytics**: Deep learning models to predict resource needs and student interest.

## Technical Milestones
- **Dockerization**: Containerized deployment for enhanced scalability and orchestration.
- **Full API Coverage**: Extending the current whitelisted APIs to cover every functional corner of the system.
- **Community Contributions**: Open-sourcing the core library module to foster community growth and innovation.

Maxedu's future scope is designed to stay ahead of the evolving needs of educational institutions.
