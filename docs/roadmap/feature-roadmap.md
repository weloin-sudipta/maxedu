# Feature Roadmap

Maxedu's development plan is divided into distinct phases, each adding significant value to the library ecosystem.

## Phase 1: Core Lifecycle (Completed)
- **Library Catalog**: Searchable and browseable book list.
- **Request & Approval**: Basic workflow for members and staff.
- **Issue & Return**: Functional endpoints for staff to record transactions.

## Phase 2: Enhanced Operations (In Progress)
- **Smart Renewal**: Logic for renewing books with reservation checks.
- **Fine Management**: Automatic overdue calculations and tracking.
- **Analytics**: Dashboards for library oversight and trends.

## Phase 3: AI & Engagement
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Advanced multi-factor scoring algorithm.
- **Smart Discovery**: AI-driven highlighting of new and relevant resources.

This roadmap guide ensures that Maxedu's growth is both strategic and prioritized.
