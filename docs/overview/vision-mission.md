# Vision & Mission

## Vision
To become the most efficient and user-centric library management solution for educational institutions, fostering a culture of reading and knowledge sharing through technology.

## Mission
- **Innovation**: Continuously improve library operations using AI and automation.
- **Accessibility**: Ensure that every student and teacher has effortless access to the resources they need.
- **Efficiency**: Streamline administrative workflows to allow library staff to focus on curation and member support.
- **Data-Driven**: Provide deep insights into reading patterns and resource utilization.
