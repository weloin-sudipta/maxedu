# Problem Statement

Many educational institutions face challenges with traditional, fragmented library systems:

## Lack of Real-Time Information
- Difficulties in tracking book availability.
- No direct visibility for students into their borrowed books or due dates.
- Delayed notifications for overdue materials.

## Administrative Inefficiency
- Manual issuance and return processes leading to errors.
- Complicated fine calculation and collection.
- Time-consuming inventory management.

## Low Engagement
- Hard for students to find relevant books beyond basic searches.
- No personalized resource recommendations.
- Limited insight into reading trends within the academic community.

Maxedu addresses these gaps by providing an integrated, real-time, and AI-powered platform for library management.
